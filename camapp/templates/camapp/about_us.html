RollCam

Effortless Attendance Using Video Cameras

Tired of taking attendance manually? RollCam is here. Now take attendance using Video Cameras or CCTVs and dynamically keep a track of the presence of every individual in the class or office. It is free of hassle and prevents people from prematurely leaving after marking their attendance.

In large classes where the presence of everyone cannot be detected manually RollCam checks the presence of every student and notifies the instructor if any student is not detected.RollCam can be used in offices too. From morning attendance to checking whether each employee is present or not. Gone are the days when employes could take 2 to 3 hour breaks now with RollCam their superior will be informed whether they are in office or not regularly.
